# Browns Studio AaaS Outbound Copywriting Playbook

This playbook outlines highly personalized, value-first outreach strategies targeting three premium niches: **Dental Clinics**, **Aesthetic Spas**, and **Law Firms**. 

### 💡 The Core Outbound Strategy
Instead of talking about ourselves ("We are a web agency, we build chatbots..."), we lead with **a personalized simulation of their potential ROI**. The primary Call to Action (CTA) directs prospects to their industry-specific landing page where they can interact with the dynamic ROI Calculator and see exact financial benefits in under 30 seconds.

## 🛠️ Outbound Execution Best Practices
1. **Highly Personalized Fields:** Always research the prospect's exact clinic, spa, or firm name, and reference their actual specialties (e.g., "implants", "Botox", "family law") to stand out from generic automation.
2. **Follow Up Flow:** If they click the link but don't book, send a friendly WhatsApp follow-up 48 hours later:
   * *“Hola [Nombre], quería consultarte si pudiste jugar un poco con el simulador de ROI y si te pareció coherente con los números reales de tu negocio. ¡Saludos!”*
3. **Multi-Channel Synergy:** Connect with them on LinkedIn/Instagram first, then reach out via WhatsApp/Email. Referencing your connection drastically improves conversion.
